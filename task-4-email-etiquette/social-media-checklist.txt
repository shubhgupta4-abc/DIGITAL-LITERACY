Do's:
Do adjust privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts.
Do think before you post (assume future employers and professors will see it).
Do use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase professional achievements, portfolios, and academic projects.
Do respect the privacy of others by asking for permission before posting photos of them or tagging them.
Do Google yourself periodically to audit your digital footprint and see what others see.


Don'ts:
Don't post content involving illegal activities, underage drinking, or explicit material.
Don't complain or rant publicly about professors, university staff, peers, or past employers.
Don't share sensitive personal information (like your home address, class schedule, or phone number) publicly.
Don't engage in cyberbullying, hate speech, or heated, unprofessional arguments online.
Don't assume "disappearing" content (like Stories or Snaps) is truly gone; screenshots last forever.
